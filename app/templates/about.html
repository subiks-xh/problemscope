About ProblemScope

Our Mission

ProblemScope is a community-driven platform designed to identify, document, and solve real-world problems through collaborative effort.

What We Do

  • Problem Identification: Users can report issues in their communities across categories like Environment, Education, Health, Infrastructure, and more.
  • Community Verification: Problems are verified by multiple users to ensure authenticity and prevent spam.
  • Collaborative Solutions: Anyone can propose solutions, which are voted on by the community to identify the most feasible approaches.
  • Impact Tracking: Our unique impact score algorithm prioritizes problems based on severity, affected population, and urgency.
  • Progress Monitoring: Track problems from identification through verification, solution proposal, implementation, and final resolution.

How It Works

1. Report

Post a problem you've identified

2. Verify

Community validates the issue

3. Solve

Propose and vote on solutions

4. Track

Monitor progress to resolution

Why ProblemScope?

Unlike traditional complaint systems, ProblemScope focuses on action. We don't just collect problems—we facilitate solutions, build communities, and track real-world impact. Every problem solved, every solution implemented, and every person helped contributes to making the world a better place.
